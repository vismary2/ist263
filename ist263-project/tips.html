Budgeting Strategies

Flights

The cheapest time to buy is often 6-8 weeks in advance for domestic trips and 2-3 months in advance for international trips. Usually, midweek departures are cheaper than weekend flights.


Hotels & Stays

Use websites like Booking.Com, Hostelworld, or AirBnb to compare prices. For last-minute deals, apps like HotelTonight can save you money!


Food & Drinks

Eat where locals eat! Street food or neighborhood restaurants are often much cheaper and tastier than touristy spots.

Daily Budgets

Research the average daily cost of living in your destination. There are often websites like Numbeo or Budget Your Trip that give estimates.

Helpful Travel Apps

  • Google Maps - Navigate anywhere, even offline.
  • Duolingo - Learn key phrases in the local language.
